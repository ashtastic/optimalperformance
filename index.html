about

Mental Performance

Why? Importance

Everyone reaches a plateau; that moment in life when things appear to be in a standstill: can things get better? Is this it? Or can I give life a little more and make it even better? We set goals for ourselves, we run through obstacles, we overcome them, we achieve our objective and then we celebrate. However, sometimes, that is not the case and overcoming the challenges becomes mentally exhausting that we give up on our goal. We’ve all been there, trying not to give up, trying to talk ourselves through the tough part. Coaching can help you develop mental and psychological skills so that you can be your best athlete, live your best life and perform your best performance.

Why? Audience, Context

Coaching is for anyone who is willing to explore how to tap into his/her inner resources in order to live a self-fulfilling life. By remaining true to core values and learning how to maintain confidence when the cup looks half empty, we can explore and reach our highest potential. People of all ages can benefit from coaching, whether you are an athlete wanting to work on your mental toughness, a student searching for motivation on carrying through a tough part of life, a professional looking to become more innovative in your work, or a person trying to live a healthier lifestyle, a coach can help support you while you achieve your goals, and aid in breaking through mental challenges.

Dina Hawari | Professional Life Coach

Education

Dina is a graduate from the University of Ottawa with a Master’s in Human Kinetics concentration in Intervention & Consultation, as well as a Bachelor of Science in Human Physiology. She also completed a coaching certification from Erickson College International, in order to gain methodologies, tools and innovative skills in the art and science of coaching.

Experience

With over 270hrs of individual coaching, group and tea workshop experience, Dina has worked with a variety of clients ranging from young students searching for their best performance to retired adults looking for something more out of life. Having worked within the sports domain, such as golf, triathletes and dragon boat paddlers, and within the health field as a physical activity consultant at a family health clinic, Dina has also coached individuals in the general life-coaching realm. Passionate about helping individuals excel in their performance, whether in a sport or at work, Dina’s philosophy is about living life in line with your values and your dreams.
